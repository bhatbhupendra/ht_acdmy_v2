Principal's Message

Janak Dhami

Principal

"A visionary leader whose dedication to education inspires us all."

It is with great pride and excitement that I welcome you to Hilltop Academy! Along with the teachers and staff at Hilltop Academy, I am working hard to continue the legacy of success by building strong relationships with parents, students, and community partners. We will focus on social-emotional learning, the use of data to drive instruction, and consistent implementation of a rigorous and relevant curriculum, to ensure that every student meets their goals and has a successful school year. Students can look forward to academic experiences that will help them build relationships while making real-world connections in a safe, caring, and supportive environment. All of us at Hilltop Academy are eager and excited to have an amazing start at this institution. As your Principal, it is important to me that everyone who steps through our doors: students, parents, and teachers are excited to be here! This attitude enables us to meet the challenges of academic excellence in a positive, fun, and nurturing way. Please feel free to call or email me to discuss any questions or concerns you might have throughout the school year. I look forward to working with you as a partner in education to ensure your child's success!

Principal
